About us

Our Soldiers: Courage, Sacrifice, Peace.

Over the past two decades, Israeli soldiers have tirelessly given their lives for their country, defending it from threats and dangers. Their selflessness and spirit knew no bounds; they were willing to sacrifice everything for the peace and security of their people.

Today, as we remember their heroism, let us pray for the repose of their souls. May our prayer serve as a testimony to our deep gratitude and respect for these warriors who gave everything for Israel.

Let our prayers be a bridge between the past and the present, praising the bravery and courage of the soldiers whose sacrifices will never be forgotten, and let us ask that their spirit and memories always inspire us to strive for peace and justice.

Learn in Memory Features

Divide up Mishnayos by Perek among family and friends in memory of a Neshama.

Email Reminders

Set an end date for the learning of Mishnayos and everyone who signs up will get an email reminder a week before that date reminding them what they took on.

Mishnayos Divided by Perek

If a full Meseches is too much for a family member or friend, they can pick specific Perakim (chapters) to learn.

Personalized and Private

Create an account in memory of a loved one and send out the private link to a selection of friends and family of your choosing.

Works Great on Phones

Participants can easily select a Meseches or Perek using their phone or desktop computer.

View List of Participants

As an organizer, you can log in and view who took what Meseches and Perek. You can edit the entries and take whatever is missing.

100% Free

This website is and will remain 100% free as a chessed to the Jewish community worldwide.
